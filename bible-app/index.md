To install a Progressive Web App (PWA) on an iOS device, you must use the Safari browser, as iOS only supports PWA installation through Safari. The process is straightforward and involves the following steps:

- Open the Safari browser on your iPhone or iPad.
- Navigate to the website of the PWA you wish to install.
- Tap the share icon (a square with an upward arrow) located at the bottom of the screen.
- From the share menu, select "Add to Home Screen".
- Optionally, you can rename the app shortcut to a preferred name before adding it.
- Tap "Add" to confirm the installation.

Once installed, the PWA will appear on your home screen and can be launched like a native app. This method works on iOS 16.3 and later, with support available from Safari, Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Orion browsers on iOS 16.4 and newer.

*The PWA works on all modern browsers and can be installed to your home screen for a native app experience.*

## Features

**Read Offline**  
Download Bible translations to your device and read without an internet connection. Perfect for travel, commutes, or places with limited connectivity.

**Search Scripture**  
Quickly find verses, passages, and topics across the entire Bible with our built-in search functionality.

**Multiple Versions**  
Access and switch between different Bible translations to deepen your understanding of God's Word.

**Easy Navigation**  
Navigate seamlessly through books, chapters, and verses with an intuitive interface designed for distraction-free reading.

## Why Bible App?

- **Privacy-focused**: Your reading history and downloads stay on your device - I don't track your usage at all.
- **Lightweight**: Fast and responsive, even on older devices
- **No distractions**: Clean interface focused on Scripture alone
- **Always accessible**: Offline support means the Word is always available
